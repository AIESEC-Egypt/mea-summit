About Egypt

EGYPT CULTURE

Egyptian culture, deeply rooted in history, centers around the life-giving Nile River, the grandeur of the pyramids, and the intricate beauty of ancient hieroglyphics. The Nile has been the heart of Egyptian civilization for millennia, providing water and fertile land that supported agriculture and trade.

EGYPT HISTORY

Egypt's history stretches back over 5,000 years, beginning with the unification of Upper and Lower Egypt around 3100 BCE under Pharaoh Narmer. Renowned for monumental architecture like the pyramids and the Sphinx, ancient Egyptians made significant advancements in art, writing (hieroglyphics), and religion.
