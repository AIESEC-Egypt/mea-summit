What Is MEA Summit

Middle East and Africa Summit is a creation of a regional plenary, that would witness 150+ young professionals hailing from 55+ countries along with business leaders, entrepreneurs to enunciate the idea of peace and fulfilment of humankind's potential.


This conference marks the importance of leadership and cross cultural development by getting the entire world under one roof. AIESEC in Egypt is proud to host the Middle East and Africa Summit and is awaiting your presence in the same
